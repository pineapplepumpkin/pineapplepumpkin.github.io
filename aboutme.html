Hi there!

I’m Cat

UX designer and passionate researcher with a background in recruitment, marketing strategy and language education. My work has always centred around people: understanding what they need, creating experiences that resonate and making sure nothing feels one-size-fits-all.

That’s what led me to UX. My ‘happy place’ is user research synthesis; uncovering what’s really going on beneath the surface and using those insights to design purposeful, intuitive solutions. Whether I’m building out user flows or refining a prototype, I approach every challenge with curiosity, structure and empathy.

Before design, I ran onboarding programs at CHANEL as the primary recruiter, created content and hosted community events at a retail developer and taught English to very energetic toddlers; I bring those same skills into my design work: clear communication, thoughtful organisation and the ability to adapt quickly.

Outside of design, you’ll find me reading webtoons, dabbling in ceramics or on a never-ending quest to find the best flat white in town.
