Who I am

Hi there! I'm a Cat. I'm a UX designer and researcher. My goal when designing is to create experiences that are user-focused. User research and usability testing is my 'happy place'; getting to talk to users and really dig into what their needs, wants, and goals are is my favourite part of the process. I’m committed to continuous growth and delivering work that meets the highest standards. Whether zooming out to align with broader strategies or diving deep into the nuances, I approach every project holistically and cross-functionally. I’d like my work to connect to a deeper purpose—improving lives and leaving the world better than I found it.
